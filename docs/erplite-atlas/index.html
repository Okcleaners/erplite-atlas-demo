Who should alpha test?

Good early testers are merchants who want lower software costs, have real operational pain, and are willing to give direct feedback while the system is still being shaped.

  • Independent retail or service merchants
  • Coffee shops, boutiques, salons, med spas, repair shops, and local operators
  • Owners who want better reporting, customer context, and inventory visibility
  • Merchants open to processing with us in exchange for no monthly software fee

Who is it not for yet?

Atlas is not being sold as a full corporate ERP replacement in Alpha. Large multi-entity, heavily customized enterprise deployments come later.

  • Major corporations needing full ERP replacement today
  • Complex warehouse, manufacturing, or procurement-heavy organizations
  • Businesses that require a mature enterprise support contract immediately
